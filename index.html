About Me

Building Digital Solutions Across Industries

I am a versatile IT Coordinator and Software Developer currently serving at Atwima Nwabiagya North Education Directorate. Beyond my work in education, I develop custom software solutions for churches, retail businesses, and various organizations. My expertise spans ICT infrastructure management, database development, web applications, and comprehensive IT support services.

I build user-centered applications for diverse clients including schools, churches, retail stores, and businesses. My portfolio includes inventory management systems, church management platforms, point-of-sale solutions, student assessment tools, and school management systems. I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, C#, Visual Basic, and SQL to deliver robust, scalable solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.

Whether it's troubleshooting hardware issues, setting up networks, developing custom databases, or building complete management systems, I provide comprehensive IT solutions. My hands-on experience in network administration, system maintenance, hardware repairs, and software development allows me to serve clients across education, religious, retail, and business sectors.
